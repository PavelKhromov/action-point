Time to explore Marocco

This is how we serf in Marocco.

Information about the tour

Surfing on the ocean, hiking and climbing the highest point of the country Jebel Tubkal (4200m), oases, tent cities in the Sahara desert, medieval Marrakesh, traveling in 4x4 jeeps in the desert - in this country you can find rest and entertainment for every taste.
After the first two trips to Morocco, I wanted to return here more often. I had to make a decision and say goodbye to work in the office in order to fulfill my long-held dream of spending the whole winter on the ocean. Now I am doing tours in this country with ActionPoint.
