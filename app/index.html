About ActionPoint


Hi my name is Valerii. And here is my best team ever - Ann and Hisham.
I used to live in Poland and work in office for the last 7 years. After came a feeling that it was the time to change it and follow a dream. It was beginning of 2018 I open myself Morocco for the first time and got a filling of my second home. Untill March 2019 I made over 10 solo and group trips to this country and after that I decided move to Morocco and have my own place here.

Since now ActionPoint Surfhouse is our place and we enjoy to make your vacations full of new emotions and discovers. Morocco is just the right country to feel the freedom and take a rest from the rush of European lifestyle.

ActionPoint is about the open minded people stay and travel with us. We have so many stories to share and so many beautiful spots to show you in Morocco! We know we see u soon;)
